DIRGANTARA

Pengenalan Sidang

Dirgantara combines dirgha (vast) and antara (space), forming a term that signifies “the vast expanse of the sky” or the modern concept of aerospace. At its centre is a fierce, stylised Garuda Azura rendered in deep blue tones, its wings spread wide to symbolise dominance of the skies. The eagle’s sharp golden beak and intense red eye reinforce qualities of courage, agility, and unwavering focus traits embodied by the Dirgantara and its mascot, Garuda Azura. Their spirited cheer, “Dirgantara! Terbang Tinggi!” encapsulates their determination to rise and excel. The three stars below represent Sidang Three, symbolising its identity, unity, and distinguished position within the cohort.
