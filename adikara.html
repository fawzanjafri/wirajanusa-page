ADIKARA

Pengenalan Sidang

Adikara, built from adhi (supreme) and kara (holder or doer), conveys the idea of “supreme authority” or “one who holds great power,” frequently associated with rulers or figures of high command. The creative integration of the letters W and N represents the official name of the DPA 2025/2026 Cohort, Wiraja Nusa, harmoniously combined into the form of a keris, symbolising the cohort’s identity of courage and indomitable spirit. The shield signifies the strong mental and physical resilience possessed by every member of the cohort, each prepared to face any challenge as a PTD cadet. The keris hilt, stylised in the form of the number 2, denotes Sidang Dua. The incorporation of floral elements alongside the keris reflects the balance between strength and grace expected of a leader. The tengkolok, inspired by classical Malay heritage, embodies refined decorum coupled with resolute bravery.
