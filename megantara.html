MEGANTARA

Pengenalan Sidang

Megantara derives from mega (cloud) and antara (space), signifying “the realm of the clouds,” a symbol of lofty ambition. Its mascot, Bumblebee represents “Unbearably Powerful”, the spirit of defying limitations and rising beyond expectations, aligning with Megantara’s motto, “Terbang Tinggi, Bisa Tak Terperi!”.” The Megantara emblem features a robotic warrior-like face in yellow, grey, and black, adorned with a traditional headpiece and framed by two crossed kris-inspired blades. Set against a golden patterned circle with strong, confident typography, the emblem reflects strength, heritage, and unwavering resilience.

"Unggul Sahsiah Sentiasa Juara"
