NAGASASRA

Pengenalan Sidang

Nagasasra is derived from naga (dragon) and śastra (weapon), literally meaning “the dragon weapon.” Nagasasra is inspired by the legendary Javanese keris Nagasasra, famed as one of the most powerful and sacred blades in Malay–Nusantara heritage. Symbolising wisdom, courage, and unbreakable spirit, it embodied the strength of a dragon (Naga). Carrying this legacy, Team Nagasasra stands for excellence, resilience, and unity. Just as the keris was forged through fire and precision, this team is shaped by determination, discipline, and high performance. With that, team Nagasasra is ready to excel in every challenge and rise above the rest.
